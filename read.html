Tips and Tricks

Welcome to Just Read! Double click on a sentence to save your location on the page. You will be presented with 2 options: You will be able to copy a special URL to your clipboard, or you can have the page automatically save that URL to your browser's history where you can find it later. Opening the URL will will start you off with that sentence right at the top of the page.
